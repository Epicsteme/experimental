Sociology of Rocket League


Being able to read a situation and adapt to it is a priceless skill. I often experimented playing Rocket League with the chat feature being disabled in Doubles Mode (2v2). Though communication plays an important role, I often found myself and my teammate performing quite well as a team without relying on chat. We read each other, the ball, the opponents, and the field in order to anticipate and coordinate to achieve common goal (pun unintended).

It is a delight to successfully coordinate with a complete stranger to work towards a common goal and succeed at it without even saying a single word to each other.
