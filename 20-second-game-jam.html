20 Second
Game Jam


Introduction

The 20 Second Game Jam ran from 5 November to 6 December 2022, and over 500 games were submitted to the jam. Since developers have a very short amount of time to grab the player's attention, this jam allowed the developers to practice building 20 seconds of fun that would leave the player wanting more. The jam was open to everyone and there were no prizes, judges, or ratings. The sponsor of the jam, DragonRuby Game Toolkit, gave away free, full, standard licenses. Those who submitted games made with DragonRuby were offered DragonRuby Pro Lifetime Licences.
